A Few Facts

  • 61,500,000 americans suffer from a mental health disorder every years. That's one in four adults
  • 70-90% of people with metnal illness saw improvement in their quality of life after having some sort of treatment
  • 16 out of 100,000 people commit suicide or one death every 40 seconds
  • By 2020 the death rate will increase to one every 20 seconds
  • Mental health disorders are usually associated with more than 90% of all suicide cases
  • Leading cause of death in the USA
  • Suicide is the 2nd cause of death for individuals among 10 and 34 years old
  • Suicide rates have increased 28% since 1999 to 2016 and is still rising
  • Statistically more men than women commit suicide in the USA(its about 4 times more than women)
  • Only half of all Americans experiencing an episode of major depression receive treatment
  • 80%-90% of people that seek treatment for depression are treated successfully using therapy and/or medication


Home

Life can be depressing sometimes; as founders, we understand that. In many circumstances, people just want the ability to rant about what troubles them. We have grown up in a generation that fully depends on technology; therefore, dealing with emotions is something new and intimidating. When not interpreted properly, these emotions become dangerous and overpowering. This can be an even bigger problem when the danger is brought upon you by a loved one; a bully; a stranger. As people, we have to keep in mind that words do hurt. They have the power to burrow into one's skin and accumulate into tumors; a cancer that feeds off depression. Instead of acting on instinct, we should gather for the greater good and spread the word of happiness. Whether it's listening to a friend, writing your thoughts, or thinking before speaking, we can all help stop abusive scenarios. For ourselves and others around us.
